## Profiles

Valid JFR snapshots:

- `jfr-search-2026-06-11/searchengine.benchmark.QueryPipelineBenchmark.execute-AverageTime/profile.jfr`
- `jfr-index-build-optimized-2026-06-11/searchengine.benchmark.IndexBuildBenchmark.build-AverageTime/profile.jfr`

Search samples are concentrated in posting-list `advance`, AND, OR and
positional merging. The initial build profile exposed boxed positions,
per-term TreeMaps and regex snippet normalization; all three were removed,
then the optimized build profile was recorded.

async-profiler is not installed in this environment, so no HTML flame graph
was generated.
